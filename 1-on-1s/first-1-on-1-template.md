# The Initial 1-on-1: Establishing the Operating Manual

**Purpose:** The first 1-on-1 establishes baseline psychological safety and operating parameters. It is not for project updates; it is to understand how we can build a high-performing partnership.

## 🤝 1. Career Journey & Motivators
* Walk me through your journey. What brings you the most energy in your day-to-day work?
* What are the primary motivators for you right now? (e.g., deep technical mastery, scope/leadership, work-life boundaries, specific tech stack exposure like AWS/Kubernetes).

## ⚙️ 2. The "Operating Manual"
* **Feedback loops:** How do you prefer constructive feedback? (Written async, verbal in 1-on-1s, immediate Slack message?)
* **Recognition:** Do you prefer public praise (e.g., in team channels/all-hands) or private acknowledgment?
* **Deep Work:** When are your optimal focus hours? How can I best protect your calendar from context switching?
* **Communication:** What is your preferred medium for complex architectural debates vs. quick unblocking? 

## 🎯 3. Mutual Expectations
* **What you can expect from me:** Radical candor, clearing blockers, shielding the team from top-down noise, and providing business context for every technical initiative.
* **What I expect from you:** Proactive communication on blockers, ownership of your deliverables from local dev to production, and a commitment to our team's engineering standards.
* What is one trait from a previous manager that you found incredibly valuable, and one trait that actively hindered your work?
