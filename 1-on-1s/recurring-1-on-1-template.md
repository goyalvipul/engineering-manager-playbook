# Recurring 1-on-1 Framework

**Frequency:** Weekly (30 mins) or Bi-weekly (45 mins)
**Format:** Direct report owns the agenda. Must be populated 24 hours prior to the sync.

## 🌡️ 1. Health & Alignment (5-10 mins)
* **Traffic Light Check-in:** (Red/Yellow/Green) on personal bandwidth and project health.
* Are you feeling challenged? Are we balancing your technical debt work with feature delivery effectively?

## 🚧 2. Execution & Blockers (10-15 mins)
* What is the biggest risk to your current sprint deliverables?
* Are there cross-functional dependencies (Product, Data, DevOps) that I need to escalate or unblock for you?
* *Optional Tech Deep Dive:* Are there any architectural decisions or PRs you want to whiteboard or discuss right now?

## 📈 3. Continuous Feedback (5 mins)
* **Manager -> Report:** Specific, actionable observation from the past week (e.g., "Your handling of the DynamoDB scaling issue in the RCA was excellent, but let's ensure we document the runbook update").
* **Report -> Manager:** Where did I fail to support you this week? What could I be doing better?

## 🚀 4. Career Progression Tracking (5 mins)
* Review progress against the established growth plan. 
* Identify upcoming projects or stretch assignments that align with the next level in the engineering matrix.
